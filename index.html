Genki Online

Genki Online (English)

What's Genki?

Genki is an integrated textbook series in elementary Japanese that is widely adopted by colleges and high schools in Japan and throughout the world. The series' well-balanced approach develops the four basic language skills of speaking, listening, reading, and writing to cultivate overall language ability. (Source: Genki Online - General Introduction).

Japanese Language Proficiency Test

Lehman College is the official testing site of the Japanese Language Proficiency Test

JLPT - Japanese Language Proficiency Test

The Japanese Language Proficiency Test is administered worldwide by the Japan Foundation and Japan Educational Exchanges and Services since 1984. In the United States, the test is administered on the first Sunday of December.

For more information, please visit the Japan Foundation Website at: JLPT Japanese-Language Proficiency Test
